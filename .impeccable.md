## Design Context

### Users
Business + bleisure travelers planning short city stays where meetings and leisure must fit together efficiently.

### Brand Personality
Premium, editorial, refined. The product should feel confident, composed, and practical.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light mode-first experience with elegant typography, restrained warm palette, and asymmetrical layout tension between city selection and chat workspace.

### Design Principles
- Keep planning flow frictionless: select city, ask question, get practical output.
- Prioritize clarity and hierarchy over decorative novelty.
- Use visual restraint to signal trust and quality.
- Make each city feel intentional through contextual prompts.
- Preserve accessibility and legibility across device sizes.
